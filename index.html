Joining ForgeServ Servers

Generally only friends or friends of friends are ever invited to any / all of the ForgeServ servers. It's not a strict rule, but I have a Minecraft playing style that I prefer (e.g. no cheating / pranking, etc.) that I prefer everyone else stick with as well. If you really want to join give me (@oitsjustjose) a shout on the Discord . We'll discuss it at that point.
